Video Tutorials




In this video, I am demonstrating how to get the grassy effect when using acrylic paints. I am using a Master's Touch size 0 Rubber Point brush and the paint colors black, white, Christmas red, spiced carrot, bright yellow, and espresso bean brown. On top of the black underlay, I started making thin strokes with espresso bean brown. Then, I made the same strokes on top of that with Christmas red. I did the same thing with spiced carrot and bright yellow. After finishing with all of those colors, I went back through and fixed any strokes that appeared too thick with the black and espresso brown. That is how I achieve the look of grass!
