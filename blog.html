Tranquility at the Lake

This past week, I finally finished up a painting that I have been working on for a while! I started it probably close to two years ago, but it just kept getting pushed behind other orders that I was doing. I finally found the time to work on it, despite the craziness of finals week approaching. I was actually inspired for this painting by one I found on Pinterest a while back. I made quite a few changes to it, but I loved the colors and vibrance of it! Now onto my next project!

Sara Jenkins - May 6, 2020
